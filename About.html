About Cash Flow Ledger

What is Cash Flow Ledger?

The Cash Flow Ledger is a web application designed to help you manage your finances efficiently. It allows you to track your income and expenses, set budgets, and visualize your financial health through interactive charts.

How to Use the Cash Flow Ledger

Step 1: View Your Account Balance

When you first open the app, you will see your account balance at the top. This amount reflects your total income minus your expenses.

Step 2: Add Your Income

To add income, click on the "Income" tile. A popup will appear where you can enter the amount and select the date of the income. After filling in the details, click "Save" to record your income.

Step 3: Add Your Expenses

Similar to adding income, click on the "Expense" tile to enter your expenses. Fill in the amount and date in the popup, then click "Save" to track your spending.

Step 4: Set a Budget

You can set a budget for either a monthly or weekly timeframe by clicking on the budget tile. Input your desired budget amount in the popup and click "Save" to help you manage your spending effectively.

Step 5: View Transactions

The recent transactions section displays a list of your income and expenses. This helps you to keep track of your financial activities over time.

Step 6: Analyze Your Finances with Charts

The application provides visualizations of your income and expenses over the week. You can see trends and patterns in your financial activities, allowing you to make informed decisions.

Step 7: View Reports and Reset

Click on "View Report" to see a summary of your financial activities. If you want to start over, use the "Reset All" button to clear all saved data and reset the app.

Why Use Cash Flow Ledger?

Managing finances can be overwhelming, but with the Cash Flow Ledger, you can take control of your financial situation. By tracking your income and expenses, setting budgets, and analyzing your spending habits, you can work towards achieving your financial goals.
